\# SYSTEM OVERVIEW



GROFIL ERP is a specialized enterprise system designed for managing

Sabra thread production, distribution, and retail operations.



The system is based on real-world workflows used in Moroccan sabra

trading and manufacturing.



It is designed to operate as both:



Internal ERP for GROFIL



and



Commercial SaaS platform (SabraFlow)



for sabra businesses worldwide.



---



\# CORE INDUSTRY



Sabra Thread Production \& Distribution



The system supports businesses involved in:



Sabra thread manufacturing  

Wholesale sabra trading  

Retail sabra sales  

Sewing accessories trading  



Typical customers include:



Traditional tailors  

Sefifa makers  

Braid artisans  

Embroidery workshops  



---



\# BUSINESS STRUCTURE



Each company may operate multiple branches.



Example:



Wholesale Sabra Branch  

Retail Sabra Branch  

Sewing Supplies Branch  



Each branch operates independently.



Branches have:



Separate inventory  

Separate cash registers  

Separate customers  

Separate suppliers  



Inter-branch movement is recorded as sales transactions.



---



\# MAIN BUSINESS UNITS



The system contains four major operational modules.



Sabra Wholesale



Manages bobines, kilograms inventory, supplier purchases,

and wholesale sales.



Sabra Retail



Handles ounce-based retail sales to tailors and artisans.



Production Workshop



Tracks workers producing sabra from bobines into retail units.



Sewing Supplies



Manages general sewing accessories and tools.



---



\# COLOR INTELLIGENCE



Color management is a core feature.



Colors are organized in a hierarchical structure:



Color Family



Example:



Red  

Blue  

Yellow  



Each family contains multiple shades.



Each shade has:



Color code  

Hex color reference  

Inventory tracking  



The system tracks demand patterns for each color.



---



\# INVENTORY STRUCTURE



Inventory is managed per branch.



Wholesale branch stores:



Bobines  

Kilograms  



Retail branch stores:



Ounces  



Supplies branch stores:



Accessories and tools.



Inventory movements are generated from:



Purchases  

Production  

Sales  

Manual adjustments  



---



\# PRODUCTION MODEL



Production converts bobines into retail sabra units.



Workers operate production sessions.



Each session contains:



Worker  

Color  

Quantity produced in kilograms  



Supervisor approval finalizes production and updates inventory.



---



\# SALES MODEL



Sales are handled through invoices.



Invoices contain:



Customer  

Items  

Prices  

Payment information  



Invoices may be:



Paid  

Partially paid  

Deferred  



Payments may be made via:



Cash  

Cheque  

Bank transfer  

TPE  



---



\# ACCOUNTING MODEL



The accounting system is journal-based.



Every operation generates accounting entries.



Supported reports include:



General Ledger  

Trial Balance  

Balance Sheet  

Profit and Loss  



Customer and supplier balances are automatically tracked.



---



\# CHEQUE MANAGEMENT



The system manages cheque portfolios.



Cheques may pass through states:



Received  

Deposited  

Cleared  

Returned  

Endorsed  



This reflects real financial practices used by Moroccan traders.
